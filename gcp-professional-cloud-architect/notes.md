Cloud - Key Terminology to Remember

- Elasticity
  - Ability to automatically scale resources up or down based on demand
- Agility
  - Ability to move fast and respond quickly to business needs
  - Quickly deliver software
  - Quickly provision infrastructure
- Global Reach and Geo-Distribution
  - The ability to expand to new markets and deploy applications to data centers around the world quickly
- Availability
  - Are applications available when users need them
- Latency
  - Time taken for a request to travel between user and server
  - Reduced by serving users from nearby locations
- Capital Expenditure (CapEx)
  - Large upfront investment
  - For example, in infrastructure like servers and data centers
- Operational Expenditure (OpEx)
  - Ongoing cost for renting resources (or services) as needed
- Pay As You Go
  - Trade capital expense (CapEx) for variable expense (OpEx).
  - Pay only for the resources you actually use
- Economies of Scale
  - Buying resources in large quantities lowers the cost per unit
- Avoid Undifferentiated Heavy Lifting
  - Spend more time on what makes your business unique
  - Example: Insurance company focussing on calculating better premiums
